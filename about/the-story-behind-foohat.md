## 1. What inspired me to build Foohat?

The inspiration came from watching my sister struggle with her salon business. She's an incredibly talented stylist, but I saw her spending more time juggling phone calls, managing appointments on paper, and chasing no-shows than actually doing what she loves—making clients feel beautiful. 

One night she told me, "I work 12 hours a day, but I feel like I'm always behind." That hit hard. I realized thousands of salon owners were in the same boat—passionate professionals trapped by administrative chaos.

---

## 2. What problem were I trying to solve?

The beauty industry has a massive operational gap. **70% of salon owners spend more time on admin than on their craft.** They're dealing with:

- Endless phone calls for booking (even during client appointments)
- Double-booking nightmares that frustrate clients
- Up to 25% no-show rates eating into revenue
- Customer data scattered across spreadsheets and sticky notes
- Zero visibility into business performance

These aren't just inconveniences—they're growth killers. Talented professionals were burning out not because they lacked skills, but because they lacked the right tools.

---

## 3. How did my approach or process evolve while working on Foohat?

We started with a simple booking calendar, thinking that was the core problem. But after interviewing **200+ salon owners**, we realized they needed much more than just appointment scheduling—they needed a complete business operating system.

The biggest evolution was shifting from **"feature-building" to "problem-solving."** Instead of adding every feature competitors had, we focused on what actually moves the needle: reducing no-shows, saving admin time, and increasing revenue. 

Here's what we delivered:

| Feature | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Automated reminders** | Cutting no-shows by 25% |
| **24/7 online booking** | Clients book anytime, anywhere |
| **Real-time analytics** | Business insights at your fingertips |
| **Mobile-first design** | Manage on the go |

Another key shift: we prioritized **mobile-first design.** Salon owners are always on their feet, not sitting at desks. Every feature had to work beautifully on a phone. This wasn't just about convenience—it was about matching how they actually work.

Most importantly, we learned that **technology alone isn't enough.** That's why we added:

- Dedicated customer success managers  
- Live human support

The best software is useless if you can't get help when you need it.
