## Key Features and Benefits

### Online Booking System

Foohat's **online booking software** empowers your clients to schedule appointments 24/7 through a seamless, mobile-friendly interface. Key features include:

- **Online Booking Page**: Create a professional, branded booking page that integrates with your website and social media channels
- **Client Portal**: Give clients access to their appointment history, preferred services, and personalized recommendations
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Build trust and attract new clients with automated review collection and display
- **Gift Cards**: Boost revenue with digital gift card sales and redemption tracking
- **Loyalty Program**: Reward repeat customers and increase retention with customizable loyalty points
- **Referral Program**: Turn satisfied clients into brand advocates with automated referral tracking and rewards

The online booking system reduces no-shows by up to 30% through automated SMS and email reminders, while increasing conversions by making it easy for clients to book anytime, anywhere.

### Smart Scheduling Software

Foohat's **intelligent scheduling system** eliminates double bookings and manual coordination headaches. The platform offers:

- **Smart Calendar View**: Visualize appointments across multiple staff members, services, and locations with color-coded scheduling
- **Staff Scheduling**: Manage employee availability, working hours, and time-off requests with drag-and-drop simplicity
- **Conflict Prevention**: Automatic detection and prevention of scheduling conflicts before they occur
- **Service Duration Control**: Set specific service durations and buffer times to optimize your daily schedule
- **Inventory Tracking**: Monitor product usage and stock levels in real-time to avoid shortages
- **Point of Sale (POS)**: Process payments, manage tips, and track sales directly within the platform

With Foohat's smart scheduling, salon owners save an average of 10+ hours per week on administrative tasks, allowing them to focus on what matters most: serving clients and growing their business.
